About Natural Galapagos

Natural Galapagos, founded by Vanessa Barona, is dedicated to sustainability and environmental protection. Every product is handmade from scratch by Vanessa herself, using high-quality, imported braided and combed cotton that is untreated and recycled. This ensures our eco-friendly creations align with a zero-waste approach and sustainable fashion.

Handcrafted in Santa Cruz, Galapagos, our recycled cotton bags and other offerings not only reduce environmental impact but also celebrate local craftsmanship and dedication to sustainable living. Natural Galapagos brings eco-friendly solutions directly to your doorstep, supporting a greener, more conscious future for all.
